Research Fellow in Polymer EOR
Heriot-Watt University


I worked with Mo during his thesis project during his MSc studies at Heriot Watt University. He was a greatly motivated student who took a great interest in the topic, delivered a great thesis and used his own knowledge to drive the direction of the project. He quickly took to the use of thermally activated polymers as in-depth conformance control, using cutting edge reservoir simulation software to show a optimal design (in both recovery and NPV) and suggest new methodologies for designing treatments. It was a pleasure to work with Mo for his thesis project and I am sure he will excel at his next venture as well.
