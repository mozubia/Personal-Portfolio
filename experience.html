Master's Design Project

Heriot-Watt University

1 Jun - 21 Jul 2020

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this project was carried out remotely during my MSc studies.


In the Field Development Project (FDP), students were given the opportunity to apply the knowledge acquired during the taught courses to a realistic industry problem. The project was carried out in teams of 5 or 6 students. The teams had to operate as project or asset teams, as would be the case within an oil company.


Each team was provided with data from several wells relating to an oil discovery. A technical and economic analysis leading to development of the prospect was to be carried out, taking into account the Government’s energy and environmental policy. The conclusions were presented to Management and Government representatives for approval.


This project entailed the combined use of: Petrel; Eclipse100; WellFlo; Techlog; MATLAB; and Excel.
